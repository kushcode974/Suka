KalanTark: The Modern KaalChakra Constitution

I. Founding Principle
KalanTark is the sovereign economic system of time. It governs the life of the visionary who refuses to be ruled by external validation, distraction, or societal templates. Time is not spent — it is invested, taxed, audited, and compounded.
"Time is not ticking. It's negotiating."
— Constitution of KalanTark

II. The 7 KalanSutras (Laws of Time Economy)

1. Every Minute = 1 KT (KalanTark Token)
Treat time as currency. Each minute is a sovereign token. There is no free time — only poorly invested time.

2. No KT Shall Be Wasted Without Audit
Every KT wasted must be tracked, reviewed, and reasoned. A minute of laziness must be taxed with reflection.

3. KTs Must Flow Toward the Destiny Core
Highest ROI zones include: Vision Work, AI-Building, Defense Learning, Bhagavad Gita Study, Body Training, Emotional Mastery, and Empire Systems.

4. Sweet Distractions Are Black Market Trades
Cute talks, relationship fantasies, CGPA obsessions, and endless scrolling are illegal trades in the KalanTark system.

5. Bonus KPZ (Kalan Productivity Zones)
When flow state hits, activate Bonus KPZ mode. Each KT here has 3x value. Track and harness it.

6. Emotional Leaks = KT Bankruptcy
Rage, revenge, self-pity, overthinking — these are emotional leaks. Patch them with system loops and detachment rituals.

7. Bhagavad Gita Is the Central Bank
When the time economy crashes (low energy, confusion, temptation), return to the Gita. All mindset liquidity flows from here.
